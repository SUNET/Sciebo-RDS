Sciebo Research Data Services

Your sciebo extension for managing research data

Sciebo Research Data Services is a non-commercial service for researchers, students and teachers improving the management of your research data without the need of additional tools or programs.
Sciebo RDS integrates into researchers' workflows by adding new functionality within sciebo. Research data management and scientific analyses based on research data thus become even easier and smoother.

Features:
  • Easily publish your datasets or supplemental materials to Zenodo or OSF.
  • Increase the visibility of your research and your citations by generating a DOI for your datasets.
  • Easily comply with the requirements of the DFG, the EU and other project sponsors or journals.
  • Compliance with FAIR Data principles - contribute to Open Science!
  • No additional account or tool necessary.
